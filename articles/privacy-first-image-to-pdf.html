The Hidden Risk of Online PDF Converters

Many online PDF converters work by uploading your files to remote servers. Once uploaded, you have no visibility into how long files are stored, who can access them, or whether they are logged or analyzed.

  • Uploaded files may be stored temporarily or permanently
  • Documents can be scanned or indexed
  • Personal or business data may be exposed

What Does Privacy-First Mean?

A privacy-first PDF tool is designed so your files never leave your device. All processing happens locally, inside your browser, using modern web technologies.

  • No upload PDF conversion
  • No server-side processing
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No ads or hidden scripts

Client-side PDF conversion uses JavaScript and HTML5 to generate PDFs directly in your browser. This approach eliminates the need for servers entirely.
